# INSURANCE-RISK-CLAIMS-ANALYSIS-PowerBI-Dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing insurance policy distribution, claim behavior, and customer demographics. It explores patterns across vehicle type, age group, education, coverage zone, and marital status to identify risk segments and monitor claim trends, helping insurers make data-driven decisions.
Key Business Objectives:- 
Analyze total insurance policies and claim amounts
Identify risk patterns across customer demographics
Understand policy distribution by vehicle usage and make
Evaluate claim trends across different education and marital statuses
Monitor policy growth trends over time
